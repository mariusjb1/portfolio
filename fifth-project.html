Extracurricular Project

Warwick Racing are a cross-disciplinary team of tenacious engineers fixed on one goal: producing a high-performance, single-seat racing vehicle worthy of the Silverstone Circuit, and other iconic circuits worldwide. We are the University of Warwick’s Formula Student Team. During the second and third years of my degree I was involved in the Mechanical Team of project whereby I helped design and manufacture parts of the vehicle. In fourth year I was Head of Marketing & PR for which I managed a group of students, producing social media posts, newsletters, a new website and a high quality testing video.
